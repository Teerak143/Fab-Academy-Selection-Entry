Interactive Environment Optimizer

The Interactive Environment Optimizer is a smart setup designed to fix a common problem: how a workspace—like a messy desk or a stuffy classroom—affects our focus. Instead of having to stop working to adjust a lamp, turn on a fan, or close the blinds, this system does it for you by "balancing" the room automatically.

The project uses a network of micro-sensors to keep track of three main things: light levels, temperature, and noise. When the system detects that something is off—for example, if the sun goes down and the desk gets too dark—it doesn't just flip a switch. It uses logic to gradually dim the LEDs or move a motorized blind to hit the "perfect" brightness.
